Welcome One and All!



Greetings traveler young and old, have you ever wanted to let your creative side go wild?

Do you hate reading? Endless pages of rules feel overcomplicated and tiresome? Do you want to just get right into the fun?


Well do I have something for you!


I am offering free lessons in any of the three TTRPG's that I have a mastery over. Be it the well known classic know as DND, or smaller more niche TTRPG's like Cyberpunk Red or JOJO TTRPG. I will gladly sit down and teach you all of the ins and outs of the TTRPG of your choice so that you can skip the boring reading and get right into the creative fun!
