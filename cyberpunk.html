Cyberpunk Red

The Cyberpunk Red TTRPG is probably on the higer scale of complex but in my personal opinion it offers some of the most intriguing options for a TTRPG that I have ever seen. Additionally the character customization is insane, you can get cyberarms, cyberlegs, cybereyes, nerual links, and so so much more. Its so expansive but can also be confusing to new players.

Now we are all wondering, how much this cost?

The book for Cyberpunk Red is free to play, just like the other TTRPG's that I am offering. Meaning that anyone can jump into the these worlds and try them out for themselves. Let that Cyber side of you manifest in this thrilling TTRPG.
