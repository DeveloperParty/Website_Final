Games


DND, otherwise known as Dungeons & Dragons is like the godfather of all TTRPG's. Starting in the late 20th century, it was seen as a satanists game that over time slowly became a game that everyone could enjoy. Its truly the bread and butter of creative TTRPG's and I'd recommend it to everyone if I could


The JoJo TTRPG is an unoffical TTRPG made from fans of the animated series. The TTRPG consists of the same freedom that the characters in the anime represent, allowing the player to express their creative talents in any way that their brain can imagine. The sky is the limit!


Cyberpunk Red was made based off the hit videogame series "Cyberpunk". It allows you to dive int the enriched dark future that is Cyberpunk. People walking around with cyberware letting them change their eye color at will or pull out a shoulder mounted rocket launcher. Think of how far technology has come, and only begin to dream of how much farther it could go.
