DND

Dungeons & Dragons is a legendary Table Top Roleplaying Game where you can live the life of an adventurer in a world crafted by your GM (Game Master). The GM like most TTRPG's has all the power of NPC's and how the world around you functions.

So what do you need to play dnd?

All you need nowadays is a DNDBeyond account. They will help you make a character and even havedigital dice to roll. If you want to get fancy you can buy books and dice but its not required. Just make a character, get a couple friends to play with you, get a GM and have fun!
